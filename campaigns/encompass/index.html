Best Practices for Encompass Performance

We help your organization to achieve optimal Encompass performance.

  • Limit the number of custom plugins being loaded*
  • Do not move loans between folders during business hours
  • Reduce document size by splitting them into two or three parts
  • Limit Alerts – Use Archive Folders
  • “Loans with Alerts” Home Page widget can cause slow loading and poor database performance
  • Watch for Reports using fields not in RDB
